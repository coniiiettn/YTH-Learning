Cocktails Favour
Gin and Tonic Light, fizzy, and a bit bitter.
Moscow Mule Refreshing and tangy, like a zesty ginger lemonade.

Mai Tai Nutty and flavorful, like a delicious mix of rum and citrus.
Paloma Bright and citrusy, tequila with a splash of grapefruit and lime.
Mimosa Bubbly and fruity, simple champagne with a hint of orange.
Class Margarita Tangy and salty, the perfect balance of tequila and lime.
Sangria Fruity and refreshing, red wine with a mix of fruity goodness.
Mojito Minty and cool, a simple blend of rum, mint, and lime.

Espresso Martini Bold and energizing, like a coffee-flavored cocktail with a kick.
Old Fashioned Classic and smooth, a simple whiskey drink with a touch of sweetness and citrus.
